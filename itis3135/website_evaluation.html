Website Evaluations

Apple's Official Website

A well-designed website that showcases Apple's products and services.

  • Contrast: The website uses contrast effectively to highlight important elements.
  • Repetition: Design elements are consistently repeated to provide a unified look.
  • Alignment: Every element is aligned, creating a clean and organized appearance.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together, making the content easy to understand.

Lings Cars

A website that is often cited as an example of poor design due to its chaotic layout and overwhelming use of graphics.

  • Contrast: The website has too many contrasting elements, making it hard to focus on key information.
  • Repetition: There's a lack of consistent design elements, leading to a disjointed appearance.
  • Alignment: Elements are scattered without clear alignment, causing visual chaos.
  • Proximity: The grouping of related elements is inconsistent, making it hard to navigate.
