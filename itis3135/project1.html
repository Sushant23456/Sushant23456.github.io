ITIS 3135 Client Project1 - Triangle Insurance

Project Overview

Application and Purpose

Application Name: Triangle Insurance Online Portal

Purpose: To provide an online platform where users can learn about various insurance products, get free quotes, and contact the insurance agent directly.

Intended Users:

The primary users of this website will be individuals or businesses looking for health, life, and tax-related insurance services.

Content Overview:

  • Home Page: Introduction to the insurance services and the agent, with calls to action.
  • Product Pages: Detailed information on health insurance, life insurance, and tax services offered.
  • About Page: Background information about the agent and the company.
  • Contact Page: A form for users to contact the agent, along with additional contact details.
  • Free Quote: Free Quotes regarding insurance policies and services.
